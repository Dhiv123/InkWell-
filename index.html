About InkWell



InkWell is a platform where creative minds can come together to
brainstorm, draft, and collaborate on writing projects.

Users can jot down their ideas in the brainstorming page,
refine them into drafts in the drafting page, set deadlines, and
share their work with the community for feedback and collaboration.

Features

Brainstorming: Generate and refine creative ideas.

Drafting: Write, edit, and save drafts.

Deadline Setting: Keep track of project timelines.

Community Sharing: Share drafts for feedback and collaboration.
